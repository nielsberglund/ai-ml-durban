Data & AI Community Day Durban: Season of AI - Copilots & Agents

Data & AI Community Day Durban: Season of AI - Copilots & Agents is a full-day, in-person event packed with cutting-edge insights and networking opportunities. It takes place place on November 23, 2024. It promises an exciting, in-depth exploration of artificial intelligence and other hot topics in the realm of Data & AI.

What to Expect

Reading the title of the event you can expect topics around Microsoft Copilot. However if you are not into Copilots (you perhaps think that Copilot is the person sitting behind the main pilot in an aeroplane), don’t worry. Since the event is organized by Azure, AI/ML & Data Community Durban we have a mix of other cool topics. So, expect topics along the lines of:

  • Microsoft Copilot Wave 2: Discover the latest advancements as Copilot integrates deeper into more Microsoft products, enhancing functionality and productivity.
  • Generative AI: Explore the transformative power of AI in content creation and innovation.
  • AI Agents & LLMs: Delve into the world of intelligent agents and large language models shaping the future of AI.
  • Database Development: Learn best practices and emerging trends in modern database management.
  • Streaming Data & Real-Time Analytics: Harness the potential of real-time data processing for actionable insights.
  • And Much More: Engage with a variety of topics that cater to all facets of Data & AI.

Even though Season of AI is a Microsoft initiative, there will be topics covering non-Microsoft tech as well. The event will feature a mix of 60-minute and 20-minute sessions, with speakers from a diverse panel of experts, architects, and Microsoft Most Valuable Professionals (MVPs) who are all at the forefront of their fields.

So, whether you’re interested in Microsoft Copilot, Azure Open AI, eager to explore the latest in Generative AI, passionate about database development, fascinated by machine learning, or interested in the world of streaming, there’s something for everyone. Our expert speakers are ready to share their insights and experiences, providing you with the knowledge and inspiration to propel your projects and career forward.
