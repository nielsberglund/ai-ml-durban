What is .NET Conf?

.NET Conf is the largest annual .NET event, hosted online by the .NET Foundation over three days (November 11-13, 2025). It’s where the global .NET community comes together to celebrate the latest innovations in the .NET ecosystem.

This year, expect major announcements around:

  • .NET 10: The latest evolution of the cross-platform framework.
  • C# 14: New language features and enhancements.
  • Performance improvements and cloud-native capabilities.
  • The future of ASP.NET Core, Blazor, MAUI, and more.

Following the virtual conference, we’re bringing the celebration to South African soil with local, in-person community editions in Durban (Nov 15), Cape Town (Nov 22), and Johannesburg (Nov 29)!

The .NET Conf Community Edition - South Africa

The .NET Conf Community Edition - South Africa is the biggest annual .NET Conference in South Africa. Following the annual .NET Conf Virtual Conference, we host uniquely South African community versions of the conference in-person and in a city near you!
