Microsoft Ignite, an annual conference for developers, IT professionals, and partners, was held in Chicago on November 18 - 22, 2024. AI dominated the conference this year, with lots of sessions about AI and new announcements of AI products. Season of AI is a Microsoft Community Pilot where Microsoft provides free content (slide decks, videos, etc.) for anyone to use at community events. Each quarter, there is a differently themed Season of AI, and given the coverage of AI at Ignite, this quarter’s theme is Season of AI - Best of AI @ Ignite.

Figure 1: Data & AI Community Day Durban: Season of AI - AI @ Ignite++

We, Azure, AI/ML & Data Community Durban, want to continue our uber-successful Season of AI events, so we are proud to announce that Season of AI - Best of AI @ Ignite is landing in Durban on March 22, 2025, in the guise of Data & AI Community Day Durban: Season of AI ‚Äî AI @ Ignite++. The “++” indicates that the event will have much more than just AI at Ignite.

Data & AI Community Day Durban: Season of AI - AI @ Ignite++

Data & AI Community Day Durban: Season of AI - AI @ Ignite++ is - as all our events - a full-day, in-person event where we deep dive into the most exciting innovations in AI, data, and cloud technologies. It is packed with cutting-edge insights and networking opportunities and promises an exciting, in-depth exploration of artificial intelligence and other hot topics in the realm of Data & AI.

What to Expect

Seeing the title of the event you can obviously expect topics around what was presented at Ignite in terms of AI. However if you are not into AI and think that Ignite is something you do with a lighter, don’t worry. Since the event is organized by Azure, AI/ML & Data Community Durban we have a mix of other cool topics. So, expect topics along the lines of:

  • AI at Ignite: Sessions covering the latest advancements in AI as presented at Ignite.
  • Generative AI: Explore the transformative power of AI in content creation and innovation.
  • AI Agents & LLMs: Delve into the world of intelligent agents and large language models shaping the future of AI.
  • Database Development: Learn best practices and emerging trends in modern database management.
  • Streaming Data & Real-Time Analytics: Harness the potential of real-time data processing for actionable insights.
  • And Much More: Engage with a variety of topics that cater to all facets of Data & AI.

Even though Season of AI is a Microsoft initiative, there will be topics covering non-Microsoft tech as well. The event will feature a mix of 60-minute and 20-minute sessions, with speakers from a diverse panel of experts, architects, and Microsoft Most Valuable Professionals (MVPs) who are all at the forefront of their fields. This time we also will have a panel discussion where you can ask questions to the speakers.

So, whether you’re interested in what happened at Ignite, Azure Open AI, eager to explore the latest in Generative AI, passionate about database development, fascinated by machine learning, or interested in the world of streaming, there’s something for everyone. Our expert speakers are ready to share their insights and experiences, providing you with the knowledge and inspiration to propel your projects and career forward.
