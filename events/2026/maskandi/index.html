So, What’s with “The Maskandi Sessions”?

If you’re from KwaZulu-Natal, you already know. For everyone else, here’s the short version: maskandi is our homegrown sound: Zulu acoustic guitar music, born right here in KZN, where one player, one guitar, and a true story is the whole show. No backing tracks. No studio gloss. No autotune. Just skill, soul, and something real to say. 🎶

Which, when you think about it, is exactly what we want from a great tech talk.

Last time, AI Unplugged was our answer to a world drowning in buzzwords: MTV Unplugged, but for tech. It worked, so we’re back for round two, and this time we’re tuning it to a Durban frequency. Same no-hype spirit, but played local: real knowledge, told straight, lessons learned (failures included), and skills you can use on Monday morning. Less “AI will change everything” hand-waving, more “here’s what actually works, and here’s how we’re doing it on this continent.”

That’s what we want to do: strip away the noise and turn up the things that matter: practical depth, local talent, and a community that lifts each other up. For the community, by the community.

(And yes, if you also read the “II” as a cheeky SQL “sequel” joke, you’re precisely our kind of people.)

What to Expect

From the title, you know there will be AI. But “Unplugged” means we go deeper, wider, and more honest than the hype cycle allows. Since the event is organised by Azure, AI/ML & Data Community Durban, we have a mix of topics celebrating everything in Data & AI, and beyond. Expect topics along the lines of:

🤖 AI & Machine Learning

  • AI Agents & Agentic AI: Multi-agent systems, orchestration, and real-world implementations
  • Generative AI: LLMs, prompt engineering, RAG, fine-tuning, and what’s actually production-ready
  • Model Context Protocol (MCP): Servers, clients, integrations, and use cases
  • AI-Coding: GitHub Copilot, Claude Code, Vibe Coding — how AI is changing the way we build software
  • Small Language Models: Running AI locally and when you don’t need GPT-scale
  • Responsible AI: Bias, fairness, ethics — the stuff that actually matters
  • AI for Africa: Unique use cases, challenges, and opportunities on our continent

📊 Data & Analytics

  • Microsoft Fabric & Analytics: Data engineering, OneLake, lakehouses, Direct Lake, and business intelligence
  • Database Development: Performance tuning, architecture, indexing, and modern database tech (because SQL is not dead, and we’ll fight anyone who says otherwise)
  • Streaming Data & Real-Time Analytics: Kafka, Event Hubs, Spark, Flink, and data in motion
  • Power Platform & Power Apps: Making AI and data accessible to business users

💻 Development & Beyond

  • .NET & Development: What’s new, what’s next, and practical developer tips
  • DevOps & Platform Engineering: CI/CD, infrastructure as code, GitHub Actions
  • Cybersecurity: Because every developer and data professional needs to think about this
  • Career & Community: Breaking into tech, levelling up your career, building your personal brand
  • Real-World Use Cases: Because theory is great, but practice is better!

The event will feature a mix of 50-minute deep dives and 20-minute lightning sessions, delivered by a diverse panel of experts, architects, and Microsoft Most Valuable Professionals (MVPs), all at the forefront of their fields. As always, we welcome topics covering non-Microsoft tech as well.

And yes, by popular demand (and a touch of infamy), the legendary panel discussion returns, ready to tackle your spiciest AI and data questions live on stage. 🔥

So, whether you’re passionate about AI agents, curious about generative AI, eager to explore data engineering, interested in database development, fascinated by .NET, or want to soak in the latest in Data & AI, there’s something for everyone.
